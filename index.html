Muhammad Mamunur Rashid, PhD

Bio: In December 2023, I received my PhD from the Computer Graphics and Vision Laboratory, under the guidance of Professor Li Guiqing, in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at South China University of Technology. After graduation, I joined the R&D department of Guangzhou GRG Wuzhou Technologies Co., Ltd. as a senior algorithm engineer, focusing on industrial scientific research in the field of artificial intelligence. I also collaborate with Professor Li Guiqing on several academic research projects. Previously, I received a Chinese Government Scholarship (CSC) and was accepted into the PhD program at South China University of Technology in September 2017. I hold a Diploma in Electrical Engineering, a Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in Electronics and Communications Engineering, and a Master of Science (M.Sc.) in Computer Science and Engineering..
