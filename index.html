About Me: I am a dedicated researcher with experience in both industrial and academic fields, passionately exploring innovative solutions and advancing knowledge. In December 2023, I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from South China University of Technology (a prestigious 985/211 institution), specializing in Computer Graphics and Vision under the mentorship of Professor Li Guiqing. Following Ph.D. graduation, I transitioned into a high-tech enterprise as a Senior Algorithm Engineer at the R&D Department of Guangzhou GRG Wuzhou Technologies Co., Ltd. , where I focus on cutting-edge AI research and development for industrial applications. Concurrently, I maintain an active academic collaboration with Professor Li Guiqing on multiple research initiatives.


My Ph.D. journey began in September 2017, when I was awarded the Chinese Government Scholarship (CSC) to pursue doctoral studies at South China University of Technology. I hold a Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in Electronics and Communications Engineering and a Master of Science (M.Sc.) in Computer Science and Engineering with a focus on computer vision.
